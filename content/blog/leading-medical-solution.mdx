### Case Study: Pre-Flight Testing with aiphrodite saves leading medical company 58% on CPP

_Note: Client details are available on request, with an NDA. For client privacy, we will refer to the client as as "ACME Co.", their agency and "Agency" and the item as "Item"._

#### Overview

ACME Co. is a leading provider for a medical solution that helps individuals manage chronic pain and improve their quality of life. The company was working with Agency for their wholistic marketing strategy and deployment. Agency was looking for audience optimization solutions and was recommended to aiphrodite as its first client. With the goal of optimizing their digital marketing strategy in particular, Agency used aiphrodite's AI Personas to understand what content would best engage their audience and thus, generate more qualified leads for the same budget.

The Q4'24 period was a critical time for Agency, as they aimed to maximize ad performance and drive significant conversions. To achieve this goal, they utilized aiphrodite as a pre-flight A/B testing solution, to identify the most effective ad creatives in the campaign. The results were nothing short of remarkable.

#### Challenge

During the most profitable quarter of the year, Agency faced the challenge of selecting the best-performing ad creatives from a pool of 14 finished assets for the time. With limited budget and time, it was imperative to quickly identify and prioritize the most impactful creatives to maximize ACME Co.'s return on ad spend. They controlled for channel factors.

#### Solution

aiphrodite, a cutting-edge pre-flight A/B testing automation solution, stepped in to address their challenge. The platform leverages advanced algorithms and data-driven insights to predict and identify the top-performing ad creatives before the campaign launch. This allowed us to make informed decisions and optimize our ad spend effectively.

#### Results

aiphrodite successfully identified two of the top ads in the campaign based off the creative. These were compared against A/B tests for the purposes of establishing the case study and results were controlled for channel. Here are the key outcomes:

- **Optimization:** The two creatives identified by aiphrodite accounted for 50% of in-platform attributed conversions.
- **Budget Efficiency:** These top-performing creatives only consumed 31% of the campaign budget, showcasing their cost-effectiveness.
- **Cost Per Purchase:** The in-platform cost per purchase for these assets was 58% lower than the campaign average.

It's evident that the assets identified by aiphrodite not only had the highest impact but also delivered the best overall efficiency.

#### Conclusion

aiphrodite proved to be an invaluable tool for Agency's marketing team. By accurately identifying the top-performing ad creatives, aiphrodite enabled Agency to allocate ACME Co.'s budget strategically and achieve outstanding results. Moving forward, Agency will continue to rely on aiphrodite to inform ad creative briefs and concentrate ad spend on the ads most likely to drive campaign success.
